# LeadFlow Pro — Market Strategy (AU & NZ Trades)

_Last updated: June 2026._

## 1. The market
- **Australia**: ~1M+ trade businesses (mostly sole traders & small crews); handyman-franchise slice alone ~A$1.4B; broader trades far larger. Skills shortage → strong demand, cashed-up but **time-poor & admin-poor** tradies.
- **New Zealand**: ~150–200k tradies; same dynamics. NZ's top lead platform (Builderscrack) is owned by Australia's hipages — the markets are converging.
- **Implication**: mature, crowded category. Win on **wedge + ease + price**, not feature parity.

## 2. Competitors

**A) Job-management software** (manage work you already have):
| Tool | Price | Weaknesses |
|---|---|---|
| ServiceM8 | from ~$29/mo | weak Android, support complaints, slows at peak, prices rising |
| Tradify | **$48–62 per user/mo** | forces its branding on invoices/emails, no offline |
| Fergus | ~$35–75/user | workflow-heavy |
| simPRO / AroFlo | $149–$200+ | commercial/enterprise; AroFlo strong on supplier/inventory |
| Ascora | ~$29+ | budget |

**B) Lead marketplaces** (sell you work):
- hipages (AU): ~$200–600/mo **+ $30–80 per lead, shared with 2–3 tradies**. Oneflare: pay-per-lead. Builderscrack (NZ, hipages-owned).

## 3. Our wedge (how we win)
1. **Lead-capture-first** — incumbents manage jobs you already won; we help **win the lead** (capture → instant auto-reply → convert). Under-served.
2. **Beat the hipages "lead tax"** — capture your **own exclusive** leads instead of paying $50 for a shared one.
3. **Flat pricing vs per-user** — $50 flat for up to 15 users vs Tradify ~$780/mo for 15. ~90% cheaper for teams.
4. **All-in-one** — leads + CRM + quote + e-sign + invoice + payment + Xero/MYOB + website + automation. Replaces hipages + ServiceM8 + a web builder.
5. **White-labelled** (Tradify can't remove its branding; we can).
6. **Built for the 70% non-techy** — done-for-you setup + AI.

## 4. Onboarding promise: live in <5 min, almost zero typing
ABN/NZBN auto-fill · import from Xero/MYOB · 1-click migrate from ServiceM8/Tradify · starter price-book per trade · SMS/phone-first · concierge for higher plans · AI setup assistant.

## 5. Scale to 50k users — honest framing
Incumbents took ~a decade to build their bases. 50k in 12 months is a **moonshot**; realistic stretch is low-thousands → 5–10k in year 1, 50k as a 2–3 yr target. Levers:
- Cheap flat pricing land-grab (watch support cost / margins).
- Referral program (trades = word of mouth).
- **Channel partnerships**: suppliers (Reece/Tradelink/Ideal), associations (Master Builders/Electricians/Plumbers), Xero/MYOB advisors, TAFE/apprenticeships.
- Anti-hipages campaign; website-plan reseller channel.
- Onboarding quality → trial-to-paid conversion is the engine.

## 6. Risks
Crowded market + entrenched trust · low ARPU + high-touch support for non-techy users · 50k/yr is aggressive · integration maturity (Meta review, live Xero/MYOB) must be solid before scaling.
